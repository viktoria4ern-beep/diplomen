Your Gym Routine is Making You Look Older

That daily HIIT workout might be chiseling your abs—but it could also be hollowing out your cheeks and adding years to your face. Dermatologists and celebrity trainers are sounding the alarm about how intense exercise accelerates aging, while smarter alternatives deliver toned bodies and glowing skin.

The Science Behind Exercise-Induced Aging

        Cortisol Collagen Breakdown

High-intensity workouts spike stress hormones that degrade facial fat pads and break down collagen. Studies show marathon runners have skin elasticity comparable to sedentary smokers.

        Repetitive Motion Wrinkles
Constant treadmill pounding creates repetitive facial tension (notice how your jaw clenches during burpees?), etching fine lines over time.

        Dehydration Damage
Sweating without proper electrolyte replenishment leaves skin papery and dull—no amount of serum can compensate.
